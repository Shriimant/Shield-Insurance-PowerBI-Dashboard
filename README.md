## Key Business Insights

### 1. Delhi NCR Generated the Highest Revenue
Delhi NCR emerged as the top performing city in terms of total revenue generation and customer acquisition, indicating strong market penetration and customer demand in metro regions.

### 2. Offline Agent Sales Mode Dominated Performance
The Offline Agent channel contributed more than 55% of total revenue, making it the most effective sales channel compared to Online App, Online Website and Offline Direct modes.

### 3. Age Group 31–40 Was the Largest Customer Segment
Customers between the ages of 31–40 represented the highest customer count and generated the largest share of revenue, making this the core business demographic.

### 4. March Was the Strongest Performing Month
Revenue trends showed a major performance spike during March, indicating seasonal growth opportunities and successful campaign performance during that period.

### 5. Senior Age Groups Had Higher Settlement Percentages
Customers in the 51–65 and 65+ age categories showed higher accepted settlement percentages, indicating increased insurance claim acceptance trends among senior customers.

### 6. Metro Cities Contributed Significant Revenue Share
Cities such as Mumbai, Hyderabad and Chennai also contributed substantial revenue, highlighting the importance of urban customer segments for business growth.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Expand Digital Sales Channels
Since Offline Agent channels currently dominate sales, the company can further increase scalability by investing in digital sales platforms such as mobile applications and online portals.

### 2. Target Younger Audiences Through Digital Campaigns
The 25–40 age segment represents the strongest customer base. Personalized digital marketing campaigns and social media engagement strategies can improve customer acquisition further.

### 3. Strengthen Marketing in Metro Cities
Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Chennai demonstrated strong revenue contribution. Increasing regional marketing efforts and customer engagement in metro cities can maximize revenue potential.

### 4. Replicate High Performing March Campaign Strategies
March showed exceptional revenue performance. Identifying the business activities, campaigns or policy offers used during this period can help replicate success across future months.

### 5. Monitor Senior Age Settlement Risks
Although senior customers have higher settlement acceptance percentages, they may also carry higher insurance risk exposure. Risk assessment and premium optimization strategies should be monitored carefully.

### 6. Improve Customer Retention Strategies
Tracking repeat customers, policy renewals and customer satisfaction metrics can improve long term business sustainability and customer loyalty.
